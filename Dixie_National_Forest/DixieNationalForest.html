Dixie National Forest



Dixie National Forest is a great place to visit. It has a lot to offer and is very unique. Many people visit the forest each year and some even return to visit. Here are some of the things people love about it:

  • Great photo spots
    ---Cascade Falls is a great photo spot, the natural red rock and flowing water can make for some beautiful pictures.
  • Rock formations
    ---Dixie National forest has red rock that looks very cool and it worth seeing.
  • Hikes
    ---If you want to see the best parts of the forest, they have hiking trails that pass through them.
  • Great views
    --- Along these hiking trails are beautiful views. They incorporate all the reasons above and make Dixie a great place to visit.

Other Activities

  • Biking
    ---Some people want to do more physical activity than just hiking, so they bring bikes. This is a great option for many people and Dixie National Forest is a great place to do it.
  • Fishing
    ---Fishing is not as common here but it is an option of something you could do during your visit.
  • Camping
    ---Lots of people also enjoy camping at Dixie National Forest. The whole area is a great place to camp, with lots of trees and some open areas.
  • Stargazing
    ---Stargazing is a very popular activity at Dixie National Forest. The area is a designated dark sky zone so you can see many stars. It is very beautiful and definitely worth seeing.
